# Music Store Database Analysis

- **Duration**: April 2024 – May 2024
- **Tech Stack**: SQL, PostgreSQL, Data Visualization
- **Description**: Analyzed customer behavior and revenue trends for a digital music store using complex SQL queries to optimize sales and marketing strategies.
- **Key Achievements**:
  - Identified top-performing genres and artists across different geographic regions
  - Analyzed customer spending patterns to identify high-value segments
  - Provided actionable insights on product performance to drive business decisions and marketing spend allocation
